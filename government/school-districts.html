The Cooperative Purchasing Program

Tel-Tec is now listed on GSA Advantage. GSA Advantage allows our School Districts, Federal, State and Local Government clients to procure our products and services directly through eBuy. eBuy is an Electronic Request for Quote (RFQ) / Request for Proposal (RFP) system designed to allow School Districts, Federal, State and Local Government clients to obtain products and services through an easy to use online portal.


Easy Online Procurement using GSA eBuy

The General Services Administration (GSA) allows only two Federal contract vehicles to be used by school districts and state and local government. One is Schedule 84, where Tel-Tec is one of only 120 companies in the Nation authorized to sell to the Federal Government and local School Districts under the same contract vehicle.

If your school district does not authorize the use of eBuy or other online procurement system, Tel-Tec will respond to your RFP through your standard process and still provide you with most favored customer pricing, guaranteed by the Government.
